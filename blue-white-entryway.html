Blue and White Entryway Styling Ideas

There is something timeless about blue and white decor. It feels fresh in spring, cool in summer and classic throughout the year.

This simple hallway combines a soft sage green console table with a blue and white vase, white hydrangeas and a framed Matisse print to create an entrance that feels calm, welcoming and beautifully put together.

The beauty of this look is that it works in almost any home. A hallway, landing, living room corner or dining room wall can all benefit from a small styled console table that provides both decoration and useful storage.

The woven basket below keeps everyday clutter tucked away while the artwork and flowers add height, colour and personality.

Why this look works

  • creates a welcoming first impression
  • combines practical storage with decorative styling
  • blue and white accessories never go out of style
  • sage green works beautifully with natural wood tones
  • easy to recreate without spending a fortune
  • suitable for hallways, landings, living rooms and dining rooms

A few carefully chosen pieces can completely transform an empty wall into a beautiful feature that feels both useful and elegant.
