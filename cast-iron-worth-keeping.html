A good cast iron casserole dish does more than cook dinner.

It holds heat, works hard and looks beautiful on a hob, shelf or table.

Why it lasts

Cast iron is made for slow cooking, steady heat and everyday use. Soups, stews, casseroles and bread all suit it well.

Why it looks good

The weight, enamel and colour add instant warmth to a kitchen. It feels useful rather than decorative.

Which colour works?

Cream feels soft and timeless. Volcanic orange is the classic. Teal adds depth. Coastal blue is lighter and more relaxed.

Worth keeping

The best kitchen pieces are used often and kept for years. Cast iron belongs in that group.
