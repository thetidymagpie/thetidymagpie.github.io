There's a particular kind of outdoor space that just works. You know it when you see it — everything has a purpose, nothing feels like an afterthought, and it looks as good at 7pm with a glass of wine as it does at midday in full sun.

This is that kind of space. A large cantilever parasol doing the serious work of keeping the sun off the seating area, a rattan bistro set for the moments when you actually want to sit upright and feel civilised, and a proper acacia wood rocking lounger for when you don't. Add a pool and you're basically on holiday.

You can't always add the pool. But you can absolutely do the rest.
