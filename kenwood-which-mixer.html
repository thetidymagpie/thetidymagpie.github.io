The best Kenwood mixer is the one that fits the kitchen and the way it will be used.

Think about storage, bowl size and baking habits before choosing the biggest model.

For small kitchens

The Kenwood Go is compact and easier to move. It suits occasional baking and tighter worktops.

For everyday baking

The kMix is a strong style choice for cakes, biscuits and regular home baking.

For bigger batches

The Chef and Chef XL make sense for bread, batch baking and family cooking.

Check the bowl size

A larger mixer is useful only if it can be stored, lifted and cleaned without becoming a chore.

For most homes, the Kenwood Chef is probably the most sensible choice. For smaller kitchens, look at the Go or kMix. For keen bakers, move up to the Chef XL or Titanium Chef Baker. Choose the mixer that fits the way you live — that is usually the one you will still be glad you bought years from now.
