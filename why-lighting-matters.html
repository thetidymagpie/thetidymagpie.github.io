Home lighting changes how every room feels.

It affects colour, texture and comfort more than most furniture does.

Use layers

Mix table lamps, wall lights, candles and soft overhead lighting instead of relying on one bright ceiling light.

Light the corners

A lamp beside a sofa, chair or shelf gives the room depth and makes evenings feel warmer.

Choose warm bulbs

Warm white light usually feels softer at home. It flatters wood, fabric, paint and ceramics.
