Garden lighting makes an outdoor space useful after dark.

It works best when the light is warm, low and layered rather than bright everywhere.

Light the edges

Use soft uplights on trees, tall grasses, walls or large pots. It gives the garden shape at night.

Add low glow

Lanterns, solar stakes and small table lights help seating areas feel inviting without glare.

Keep it warm

Warm white bulbs are usually best. They make paving, planting and outdoor furniture feel softer.
