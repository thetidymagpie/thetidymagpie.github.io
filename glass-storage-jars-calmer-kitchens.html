Glass pantry jars are one of the quickest ways to make kitchen shelves look more organised.

They reduce packaging clutter and make everyday ingredients easier to see.

Start with daily ingredients

Pasta, rice, flour, oats, coffee and cereal usually make the biggest difference.

Mix sizes carefully

Use larger jars for bulk ingredients and smaller jars for spices, seeds or baking extras.

Keep it relaxed

Not everything needs decanting. A few matching jars and a basket can be enough.
