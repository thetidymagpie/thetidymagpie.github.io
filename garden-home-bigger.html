Small garden ideas work best when the space feels intentional.

A compact patio, courtyard or narrow terrace can still feel like an outdoor room with the right pieces.

Define the space

An outdoor mat, sofa or pergola gives the seating area a clear shape.

Add height

Large pots, climbers and a simple structure draw the eye up and make the garden feel more finished.

Use warm light

Festoon lights make small gardens feel inviting after dark and help the space work for longer.
