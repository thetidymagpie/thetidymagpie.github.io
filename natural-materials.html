On the home

Natural Materials for a Warmer Home

Back to Journal

Natural materials make a home feel warmer and easier to live with.

Wood, ceramic, linen, glass, stone and clay all bring texture without needing much decoration.

Use wood for warmth

A shelf, tray, table, stool or picture frame can soften a room quickly.

Add ceramic and linen

Ceramic brings weight. Linen brings softness. Both improve with everyday use.

Choose pieces that age well

Natural materials often look better with small marks, softened edges and regular use.
