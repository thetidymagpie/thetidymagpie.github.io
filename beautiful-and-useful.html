On the home

Beautiful and Useful Home Ideas

Why everyday things deserve to be both practical and lovely — and what happens when they are.

Back to Journal

Beautiful and useful is a simple way to choose better things for a home.

The best everyday objects do their job and make a room feel better at the same time.

Choose useful beauty

A bowl, lamp, jug, tray, towel or chopping board can be practical and lovely. It does not need to be saved for best.

Buy fewer pieces

Useful things made from wood, ceramic, glass, linen or metal tend to age better than trend-led clutter.

Let things be used

Small marks and softened edges can add character. A home feels warmer when its best pieces are part of daily life.
