A Soft Bathroom with a Little Greenery

A soft, sunny bathroom look with simple wall storage, warm texture, useful details and plenty of greenery.

This bathroom look is about making practical pieces feel calm rather than cluttered. The shelves give everyday things a proper home, while the woven plant baskets, soft bath mat and greenery make the whole room feel warmer and more relaxed.

A couple of plants can completely change the feel of a bathroom. Even a small touch of greenery makes the room feel softer, fresher and more cared for. You don’t need lots of them — just one or two well-placed plants can make the whole space feel calmer and more inviting.
